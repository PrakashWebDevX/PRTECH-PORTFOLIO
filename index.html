Good to know

What is AI Agent Development? ↓

AI Agent Development involves building autonomous software systems designed to execute workflows, make decisions, and interact with users or APIs. Unlike basic chatbots, these agents can be trained on your custom documentation, integrate with your tech stack (using tools like n8n and databases), and automate complex workflows like lead qualification, scheduling, or customer support without manual intervention.

Why PRTECH.AI? ↓

We deliver premium, custom-coded websites that perform faster, rank better on search engines, and convert visitors into customers. No templates, just tailored solutions for your business.

How long does it take? ↓

Most standard projects are completed within 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the complexity and scope. Custom software or larger platforms may take slightly longer.

How much does a website cost? ↓

Our pricing is transparent and designed to scale with you. Landing pages start at ₹999, while standard multi-page websites start at ₹2,999. Custom quotes are available for complex platforms.

What technologies does PRTECH.AI use? ↓

We utilize a modern tech stack including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, robust backend systems with Python and Node.js, and deploy on reliable cloud platforms to ensure maximum uptime.

Are PRTECH.AI websites SEO-optimized? ↓

Yes, absolutely. Every website we build includes on-page SEO best practices, semantic HTML structure, and blazing-fast load times to ensure you rank well on Google.

Do you offer in-person consultations? ↓

To keep our processes highly efficient and fast, we primarily operate remotely. However, we're always available for video calls and continuous direct contact throughout your project.

Why not use a page builder like Wix or Squarespace? ↓

Page builders often suffer from slow loading speeds, generic designs, and rigid limitations. A custom-coded site from PRTECH.AI gives you complete ownership, lightning-fast performance, and a truly premium aesthetic.
